Why we are different

LinkedIn is built for search. JapanOffer AI is built for match.

Job boards show information. JapanOffer AI turns background, role requirements, language, visa and country fit into a practical match score.

Traditional job platforms

  • Users search roles by themselves
  • Users guess whether they are qualified
  • Employers receive many weak applications
  • Cross-border language and visa risk is unclear

JapanOffer AI

  • System understands the user background first
  • Roles are ranked by realistic match score
  • Employers can find better matched candidates
  • Language, visa, education and role requirements are judged together
